# Sri Lankan Legal Documents 

Legal Gazettes, Extra-Gazettes, Acts, Bills and other documents scraped from [documents.gov.lk](https://documents.gov.lk).

## Glossary 

- Gazettes: Official public journals where governments publish legal notices, regulations, and enacted laws.
- Extra-Gazettes: Special editions of the Gazette used for urgent or unscheduled announcements.
- Acts: Laws formally passed by a legislature and given legal force.
- Bills: Draft versions of proposed laws, debated in parliament before becoming Acts.
