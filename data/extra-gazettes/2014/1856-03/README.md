# [1856/03] Dept. of Labour - The Cost of Living Index Number for the Month of March 2014 applicable to workers in the Cocoa, Cardamom and Pepper Growing and Manufacturing Trade is 177.9 and the special allowance payable to workers for a normal working day in the Month of April 2014 will be Rs. 548.44
