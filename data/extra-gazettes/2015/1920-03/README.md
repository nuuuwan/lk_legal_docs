# [1920/03] Dept. of Wild Life Conservation - Order to declare that the Chundikulam Santuary as a National Reserve and be known as "Chudikulam National Park" and the state land in Delft as a National Reserve and be known as "Delft National Park" and the State Land in the Administrative Districts of Mannar, Mulativu and Vavunia as a National Reserve and be know as the "Madu Road National Park" and the State Land in Talai Mannar as a National Reserve and be known as "Adam's Bridge Marine National Park"

**Date:** 2015-06-22
