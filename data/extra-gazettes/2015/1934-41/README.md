# [1934/41] Dept. of Fiscal Policy - Order to direct that there shall be levied a duty at the rate of Rs. 1,595 per proof litre on Molasses, Palmyrah, Coconut and Processed Arrack and of Rs. 1,860 on every proof litre on Country made Foreign Spirits manufactured in Sri Lanka and imposed a duty at the rate of Rs. 190 on every litre on Malt liquor of less than 5% of absolute strength and Rs. 245 of 5% of absolute strength w.e.f. 03.10.2015

**Date:** 2015-10-02
