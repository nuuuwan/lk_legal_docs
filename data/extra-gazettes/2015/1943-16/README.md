# [1943/16] Department of Fiscal Policy - Order to direct that a duty at the rate of Rs.190.00 be imposed on every litre of Malt Liquor of less than 5% of absolute strength and Rs. 315.00 be imposed on Malt liquor of 5% and above of absolute strength and duty not be levied on any quantity issued for the use of any Diplomatic Mission if payment is made from the foreign account with effect from 05.12.2015

**Date:** 2015-12-04
