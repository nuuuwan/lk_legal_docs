# [1925/60] Department of Labour - The Cost of Living Index Number for the month of July, 2015 applicable to workers in the Cocoa, Cardamom and Pepper growing and manufacturing trade is 182.8 and the special allowance payable in respect of a normal working day in the month of August, 2015 is Rs. 567.83.
